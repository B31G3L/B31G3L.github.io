Leetspeak

is an app that can be used to translate letters into similar looking numbers.
It doesn’t matter whether a letter, a word or more is entered

There are three ways to use the Leetspeak Generator:

  • simple leet (predefined)
  • extended leet (predefined)
  • custom leet (create your own leet)
